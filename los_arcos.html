Los Arcos is a stunning natural landmark located in the central Mexican state of Querétaro. Comprising a series of towering rock formations, Los Arcos is a popular destination for nature lovers, hikers, and rock climbers. Visitors can explore the lush surrounding countryside and marvel at the sheer size and beauty of the arches, which rise high above the surrounding terrain.

Eat with an amazing view

Tucked away in the rolling hills of central Mexico, there's a restaurant that offers a breathtaking dining experience unlike any other. With its stunning views of the towering Los Arcos rock formations, this restaurant is a true gem, offering a unique blend of delicious cuisine and breathtaking natural beauty. Visitors can savor a delicious meal while taking in the stunning panoramic views of the arches, which rise high above the surrounding countryside.

Night Light Show

The night sky in Querétaro is a truly awe-inspiring sight, but add in the natural wonder of Los Arcos, and you have an experience that is nothing short of magical. The Los Arcos Night Show is a spectacular event that illuminates the towering rock formations, creating a mesmerizing light show that is both beautiful and unique. Visitors can stroll through the arches, marveling at the intricate light displays that play out against the natural stone formations
